About Us

AI Recommending Articles and Online Counselling Booking Platform is a web-based solution built to promote mental well-being and personal growth. It enables users to receive AI-driven article recommendations based on their interests and age, and also allows them to book sessions with registered counsellors easily.

The platform ensures a smooth user experience with secure login, role-based access for clients and counsellors, and a simple appointment booking system.

  • AI-powered personalized article suggestions
  • Role-based login and registration
  • Online counselling appointment booking
  • Counsellor management via admin panel
